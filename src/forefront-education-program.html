Our vision is to be at the forefront of our industry by developing the most talented professionals in the world.

Forefront is KPFF’s philosophy on education. We invest in the development of our people, openly share knowledge with each other, embrace a growth mindset, create space to discover our passions, and we always strive for excellence.

KPFF has a long history of developing talent from within. The forefront experience starts the day you walk in the door and continues as a career-long effort to grow together and advance our profession.

We recognize that formal education programs are only one component to professional development. Project-based learning, informal interactions, and self-directed learning are also essential to our development.

Formal Programs

We offer formal mentoring and training programs at the local and national level. These offerings are designed to teach a full range technical and professional skills, from analysis tools to emotional intelligence, helping people at all levels develop the necessary skills to advance in their careers.

Project-Based Learning

We learn by doing. Each day, we bring our individual and collective knowledge to our work, and, each day, we have an opportunity to learn and to teach something new. When we are mindful about embracing our curiosities, openly sharing our knowledge, and practicing a growth mindset, we create an optimal “on the job” learning environment.

Self-Directed Learning

We make space for people to follow their curiosities, develop their interests, and discover their passions. We believe when people are practicing the work they love, they thrive. And when we have a company full of people following their passions, we can change the world.

Informal Teaching Moments

Above all, we value trust and relationships. This shows up everyday in small ways across a wide network - when you swivel your chair to ask a question, or when collaborate over Teams to share insights. Trust and relationships are built through these small yet significant moments, and trust and relationships are what carry us through our careers.
