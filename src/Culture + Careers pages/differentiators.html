A united culture of excellence.

Our Core Values of Excellence, Trust, Relationships, Stability and Passion are the foundation for everything we do at KPFF. Living our values is essential to perpetuating the culture that continues to define and differentiate KPFF.

The Types of Work We Do

Our dedication to providing excellent service to our clients is a priority. That group may include architects, contractors, developers, public agencies, institutions, landscape architects or other engineering disciplines on both public and private projects. We work as subconsultants on multi-discipline teams or as prime consultants for infrastructure projects but most importantly as trusted advisors to all partners. Our projects take us to both urban and suburban environments as well as our National Parks, rural communities, and international destinations.
