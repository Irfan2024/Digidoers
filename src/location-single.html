About KPFF Los Angeles

Established in 1967, our Los Angeles office provides structural engineering, civil engineering, protective design, surveying and mapping services. We support the most exciting projects that define the region, and reshape numerous neighborhoods and communities, from the beaches on the Westside to the deserts in the East.

The City’s interesting and diverse geography provides a stunning backdrop for the vibrant downtown skyline. With the pulse of a rapidly expanding urban core, a wide variety of eclectic neighborhoods, laid back beach communities, and an epicenter of culture, entertainment and commerce, Los Angeles is a truly wonderful place to call home.

Structural Engineering

The structural engineering group takes a solutionsoriented approach to designing buildings and facilities. We collaborate with architects, builders, and owners to meet project goals. Our portfolio includes diverse projects in aviation, civic, education, government, healthcare, historic retrofits, hospitality, industrial, life sciences, data centers, offices, parking, residential, retail, sports, and infrastructure. This holistic view allows our engineers to push boundaries and achieve success

Civil Engineering

The civil engineering group designs sites for commercial, residential, institutional, industrial, and recreational use, along with essential infrastructure like energy systems, storm drainage, utility grids, and trails. We handle projects from feasibility and planning to design, permitting, and construction, collaborating closely with owners, architects, and other engineers to build sustainable, resilient communities.

Transportation Engineering

The transportation engineering group enhances community mobility for all users through sustainable infrastructure projects. We design transit systems from bus to light rail and provide comprehensive services for highways, roadways, and non-motorized transportation. Collaborating with local utility providers, we ensure utility improvements align with transportation projects to support growing communities.

Heavy Civil Structures

The heavy civil structures group designs bridge, waterfront, and marine transit projects, collaborating with public agencies on highways and ferry systems. We handle vehicular and pedestrian bridges, retaining walls, shoring, and waterfront structures like piers and terminals, providing design and inspection services for various complexities.

In addition to our engineers, our planners provide consulting services to the marine transit industry that include system, route, and terminal planning, vessel procurement, and asset management. Read more here.
